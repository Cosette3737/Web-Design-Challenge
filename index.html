Summary: Latitude vs. X

The purpose of the weatherpy project was to analyze a dataset of temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and windspeed on a random sample of locations across the globe.
The data was collected with an API and analyzed with the help of PANDAS and matplotlib. With the help of this website, the findings are easily accesible to view.
