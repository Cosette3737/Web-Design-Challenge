Max Temperature


Temperature plotted at random locations around the globe, shows a trend of warmer weather as the latitude approches 0 Degrees.
The graphed data appears to peak at the equator, or 0 deg latitude, creating a bellcurve, which indicates that temperature is dependent on latitude.
